  • Multiple note items are stacked with proper spacing

  2. ii.Public hearing and comment period
  3. iii.Final determination by City Council
WHEREAS Clauses
WHEREAS, the City of Rivergrove has authority under ORS Chapter 221 to enact ordinances for the governance of the City; and
WHEREAS, the City Council finds it necessary and appropriate to regulate certain activities to protect the public health, safety, and welfare; and
WHEREAS, these regulations have been reviewed and approved by the City Attorney;
Processing Notes
  • Letter lists are created by the postprocessor from (a) patterns
  • Definition items are detected from Section X. patterns
  • Custom numbering preserves original document numbering
  • WHEREAS clauses get special emphasis styling
  • All enhanced lists maintain semantic HTML structure
